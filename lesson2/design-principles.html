Visual Hierarchy

Apple Inc.

apple.com

The first thing we notice in this image is the iPhones, then we see the specifications in order of importance. They apply the visual hierarchy so that we see the iPhones first, which are the items the company wants us to see first followed by the specs.

Proximity

Evernote

evernote.com

We can observe two groups of elements, there is space between the groups, and in each group in which the elements are related, they are close to each other. It is a very clear example of proximity.

Contrast

We're Cinco

wearecinco.com

On this web page, they apply the principle of contrast because the colors between the background and the foreground are opposite and the readability and appreciation of the images are good.
